Project: Outdoor for you

Assignment

Making a multiple device travel guide that helps the user before, during, and after the journey.

Description

Imagine... It's somewhere in January and you finally have some free time to start thinking about your vacation in August. It's already been decided you will be visiting the Pyrenees in France this year. During your research on the internet, you find some awesome hikes and campsites and you want to save these locations. You decide to save all of them in your browser for a later time. Months later, your holiday is there and you're in your car towards the Pyrenees. After a 20 hour drive, you finally arrive in the area. But those fine campsites you had found are still saved in your browser on your desktop! Maybe you can find these campsites again if you start looking? This time though... your internet connection might not be so great. Well.. maybe 'Outdoor for you' is the perfect solution for you!

Highlights

Discover

In 'Outdoor for you', you can search for those fine campsites and awesome hikes before you go on vacation. You can save them while you're on the couch browsing through this application on your laptop. So when you arrive at your campsite after a 20-hour drive and you decide it's too crowded you can easily find other campsites near you based on your GPS location.

Weather forecast

When you are using this application on your mobile phone you will also get a weather forecast so you know when it’s a great day to start that long hike you have been saving. Or maybe it’s time to pack and move your tent towards a location with better weather

Stories

Be inspired by other nature lovers. Which awesome hikes have they discovered and what have they experienced during their trips? Or maybe you can write your own story so other users can be inspired by your travels! Show off your photographs and tell all about your highlights.

Route tracking

Are you discovering unknown terrain and do you find the most awesome hikes? Track your routes on your mobile device and share your routes with other travelers!
