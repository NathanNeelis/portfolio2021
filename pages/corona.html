Project: Corona during my study

Assignment

For In60Secons we will be making a new visualisation of (a part off) the CBS webpage filled with data about the Corona crisis. The purpose of this visualisation is to inform the reader about the complex data in a way that everyone can understand.

Description

The year 2020 has been hard on us all. The Corona Virus spread across the world in record time and effected a lot of lives. The CBS gathered a lot of information on how this effected everyoneâ€™s live in all possible ways and piled all this info onto a webpage. We processed this data and found a story about how the lives off four students has been changed.

There isn't a lot known about how the Corona virus effects the lives of student. In this web application you can read our stories on how the Corona effected our lives and our studies. Together with data visualisations our story is visualised to tell our story and inform all readers how the lives of a few students have been changed by this awful virus.

Highlights

Data visualisations

For each topic we inform the reader with data transformed in understandable visualisations.

Messages

For each topic we give insights in the lives of these four students by showing their messages related to the topic and the Corona virus.
